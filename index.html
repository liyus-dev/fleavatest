THE STAY

Stylish accommodation option at Jannata Resort & Spa incorporates a choice of 16 Deluxe Suites, 2 Deluxe Family Suite and 2 Pool Villas to anticipate all of your holiday needs. All categories are the epitome of Balinese luxury with customized interiors and essential home comforts.

During pandemic, our resort has applied health protocols to ensure that our guests are safe. Before check-in, guests are encouraged to wash their hands and our team will check guests' body temperature.

All the team members of Jannata Resort maintain the cleanliness in all areas including hotel rooms, restaurant, spa, kitchen. Every 4 hours disinfectant spray and physical distancing towards our pool decks, restaurant chairs, spa outlets are applied to ensure that our resort is in hygiene circumstance.

Masks, face shields, hand sanitizer and also washing hands counter are fully prepared to welcome the new normal era ensuring all guests and staffs are safe and healthy.
